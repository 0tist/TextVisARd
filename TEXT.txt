IIIT, Delhi (Imdraprastha Sücana Praudyögiki Samsthan Dilli,
IIIT-Delhi or IIIT-D) is a state university located in Delhi,
India. [2] It is research-oriented university with a focus on
computer science and allied areas like Computer Science and
Engineering,Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, Electronics
and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and
Design,Computer Science and Social Science and Computer Science
and Bioscience. The institute began with its first batch of 60
BTech students in 2008. IIIT-Delhi was ranked 2nd in the T
School Rankings 2019 in North Zone.
